Software Developer

This type of occupation is for coders who aspires to create different types of softwares such as the internet browser, movie player, emails, presentation, programming languages, etc. It is vital for software developers to be fluent in the programming languages they are using to write the codes needed to build their software programs. Along with the fluency in the programming languages, software programmers must attain the skill of technical creativity and organization for users to comprehend the software programs and be able to use the software programs with ease.

Education:

For the qualification background, individuals must have a bachelor's in computer science, information technology, informatics, or a related degree in this particular field with no prior experience required. However, candidates must be aware that some companies may prefer those individuals with a master's degree in the listed majors mentioned above.

Skills:

  • Mathematical Skills
  • Problem-Solving Skills
  • Knowledge in Different Types of Programming Languages
  • Time Management/Organization
  • Excellent Teamwork
  • Detail-Oriented

Growth Rate/Salary:

In this field, the growth rate is 22% and the median annual salary is $110,140 per year.

Employment:

Individuals can excel in this field in various ways. Some examples include working for tech companies in their office or relocating to a different area, or if candidates are unable to come to office or relocate, they can work remotely from home with the permission of the employer.
