Information Security Analyst:

This type of occupation is for individuals that aspire to install software such as firewalls in order to protect confidential information. Additionally, ISA are supposed to plan accordingly in order to carry out strict security measures to protect confidential information that belongs to different organization such as their computer networks and systems from hackers that can misuse this information and can put the country in danger.

Education:

For the qualification background, candidates must have a bachelor's degree in computer science, information technology, informatics, or a related degree in this particular field with less than five years of experience required. However, candidates must be aware that some companies may prefer those individuals with a master's degree in the listed majors mentioned above.

Skills:

  • Knowledge in Different Types of Programming
  • IT Knowledge
  • Analytical
  • Detail-Oriented
  • Networking
  • Strong Communication Skills
  • Excellent problem-solving skills
  • Excellent Teamwork
  • Time Management/Organization

Growth Rate/Salary:

In this field, the growth rate is 33% and the median annual salary is $103,590 per year.

Employment:

Individuals can excel in this field in various ways. Some examples include working for tech companies in their office or relocating to a different area, or if candidates are unable to come to office or relocate, they can work remotely from home with the permission of the employer.
