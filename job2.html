Web Developer

This type of occupation is for individuals who seek to design a website that contains a home page as well as making a user friendly website for users to use on their convenience without being concerned about scams. Web developers use HTML/CSS, C#, and Ruby to create these types of friendly websites. Some examples of friendly websites include National Geographic, Madewell, Wave Rider, Falve, Intersection, Squarespace, Spotify, etc. Like a software developer, they must attain the skill of technical creativity and organization for users to utlize the website to their convenience without fear of being scammed.

Education:

For the qualification background, it varies from a high school diploma to a bachelor's degree. Nevertheless, for individuals with a bachelor's degree, it can be in computer science, informatics, or a related degree in this particular field.

Skills:

  • Knowledge in Different Types of Programming
  • Strong numeracy skills
  • Strong creative ability
  • Detail-Oriented
  • Strong Communication Skills
  • >Excellent problem-solving skills
  • Excellent Teamwork
  • Time Management/Organization

Growth Rate/Salary:

In this field, the growth rate is 13% and the median annual salary is $77,200 per year.
